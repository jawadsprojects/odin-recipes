• 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
      1 teaspoon garlic powder
      1 teaspoon salt
      4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

    • Step 1 Whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together in a shallow baking dish; add chicken breasts and turn to coat. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight. Step 2 Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate. Step 3 Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess; discard remaining marinade. Step 4 Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 7 to 8 minutes on each side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
